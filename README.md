# snoozeulose

Mobile mockup MVP for a web-based gamified alarm clock. Don't hit snooze: your crew finds out.

Live demo: https://shaunduval.github.io/snoozeulose-mvp/

One self-contained file, [index.html](index.html). No build step, no dependencies. Open it in a browser at mobile width (375 to 430px).

## What it demos

- Splash screen that explains the rules: commit to a wake time, rise for points, every snooze costs 20 pts and auto-posts to your crew, leaderboard resets monday.
- A test alarm with both outcomes: tap snooze and watch the shame post hit the feed, or press and hold "i'm up" to bank the points and climb the board.
- Crew leaderboard and status feed with 4 players (tiah s., larry w., shaun d., timothy s.) and tappable reactions.
- Sleep hygiene stats: consistency score, wake-time chart, wind-down nudge toggle.

All state is in-memory. Reload the page (or tap "reset demo") to start over.
